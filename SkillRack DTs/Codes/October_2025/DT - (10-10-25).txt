Problem Statement :-

Sort Subarrays By First Integer in DT (Daily Test) in SkillRack in Python - Programming

Code :-

N = int(input())

values_list = list(map(int , input().split()))

K = int(input())

sorted_subarrays_list = sorted([values_list[index : (index + K)] for index in range(0 , N , K)] , key = lambda data_list : data_list[0])

[print(*data_list , sep = " " , end = " ") for data_list in sorted_subarrays_list]
