Problem Statement :-

Digits To Asterisks Pattern in DT (Daily Test) in SkillRack in Python - Programming

Code :-

digits_list = [int(digit_string) for digit_string in input().strip()]

R = max(digits_list)

C = sum(digits_list)

matrix = [['-' for j in range(0 , C)] for i in range(0 , R)]

column_index = 0

for digit in digits_list:
    
    i = (R - digit)
    
    [matrix[i].__setitem__(j , '*') for j in range(column_index , (column_index + digit))]
    
    column_index += digit

[print(*row_list , sep = "") for row_list in matrix]
