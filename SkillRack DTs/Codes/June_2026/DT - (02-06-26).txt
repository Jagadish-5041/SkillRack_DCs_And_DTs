Problem Statement :-

Word Train Without Fixed First String in DT (Daily Test) in SkillRack in Python - Programming

Code :-

N = int(input())

word_strings_list = [input().strip() for index in range(0 , N)]

first_alphabet_words_map = {word_string[0] : word_string for word_string in word_strings_list}

last_alphabets_string = ("".join(word_string[-1] for word_string in word_strings_list))

for word_string in word_strings_list:
    
    if(word_string[0] not in last_alphabets_string):
        
        starting_word_string = word_string
        
        break

current_word_string = starting_word_string

for count in range(0 , N):
    
    print(current_word_string)
    
    current_word_string = first_alphabet_words_map.get(current_word_string[-1] , "")
