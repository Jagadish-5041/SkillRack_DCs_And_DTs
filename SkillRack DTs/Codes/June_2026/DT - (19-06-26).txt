Problem Statement :-

Series - Digit At Nth Position in DT (Daily Test) in SkillRack in Python - Programming

Code :-

M , N = map(int , input().strip().split())

series_string = ""

for number in range(1 , (M + 1)):
    
    series_string = (series_string + str(number))
    
    if(len(series_string) >= N):
        
        print(series_string[(N - 1)])
        
        quit()
