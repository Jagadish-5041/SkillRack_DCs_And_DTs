Problem Statement :-

Hyphen Asterisk Pattern in DT (Daily Test) in SkillRack in Python - Programming

Code :-

N = int(input())

matrix = [["" for j in range(0 , N)] for i in range(0 , N)]

for j in range(0 , ((N // 2) + 1)):
    
    cell_value = '*'
    
    for i in range(j , (N - j)):
        
        matrix[i][j] = cell_value
        
        cell_value = ('-') if(cell_value == '*') else ('*')

[print(*row_list , sep = "") for row_list in matrix]
