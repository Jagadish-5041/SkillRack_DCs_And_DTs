Problem Statement :-

Sort Columns Based On Sum in DT (Daily Test) in SkillRack in Python - Programming

Code :-

R , C = map(int , input().strip().split())

matrix = [list(map(int , input().strip().split())) for i in range(0 , R)]

data_list = sorted([(sum(matrix[i][j] for i in range(0 , R)) , j) for j in range(0 , C)] , key = lambda data_tuple : data_tuple[0])
