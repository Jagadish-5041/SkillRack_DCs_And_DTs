Problem Statement :-

Pattern Printing - Mirror Image With Hyphens in DT (Daily Test) in SkillRack in Python - Programming

Code :-

N = int(input())

for number , hyphens_count in zip(range(N , 0 , -1) , range(1 , ((N * 2) + 1) , 2)):
    
    left_string = (str(number) * number)
    
    middle_string = ('-' * hyphens_count)
    
    right_string = left_string
    
    row_string = (left_string + middle_string + right_string)
    
    print(row_string)
