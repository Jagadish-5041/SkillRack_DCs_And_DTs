Problem Statement :-

Immediate Smaller To M in DT (Daily Test) in SkillRack in Python - Programming

Code :-

N = int(input())

values_list = list(map(int , input().split()))

M = int(input())

minimum_difference = M

minimum_difference_value = -1

for value in values_list:
    
    if(((M - value) > 0) and (minimum_difference > (M - value))):
        
        minimum_difference = (M - value)
        
        minimum_difference_value = value

print(minimum_difference_value)
