Problem Statement :-

Maximum Speed Of Bike in DT (Daily Test) in SkillRack in Python - Programming

Code :-

N , P , Q = map(int , input().split())

current_speed = 40

maximum_speed = 0

for speed in list(map(int , input().split())):
    
    maximum_speed = max(maximum_speed , current_speed)
    
    current_speed = (current_speed + P) if(speed == P) else ((current_speed - Q) if(speed == Q) else (current_speed))
    
    maximum_speed = max(maximum_speed , current_speed)

print(maximum_speed)
