Problem Statement :-

Street Numbers With Most Houses in DT (Daily Test) in SkillRack in Python - Programming

Code :-

N = int(input().strip())

row_wise_hyphens_count_list = [input().strip().count('-') for i in range(0 , N)]

maximum_hyphens_count = max(row_wise_hyphens_count_list)

print(*[(index + 1) for index , hyphens_count in enumerate(row_wise_hyphens_count_list) if(hyphens_count == maximum_hyphens_count)])
