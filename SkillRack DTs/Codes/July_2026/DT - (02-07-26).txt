Problem Statement :-

String Characters Mixing in DT (Daily Test) in SkillRack in Python - Programming

Code :-

characters_list_1 , characters_list_2 = map(list , input().strip().split())

for index in range(0 , len(characters_list_1)):
    
    characters_list_copy = characters_list_2.copy()
    
    characters_list_copy[index] = characters_list_1[index]
    
    print(*characters_list_copy , sep = "" , end = " ")
