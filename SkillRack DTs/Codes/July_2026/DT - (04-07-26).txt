Problem Statement :-

Count Of Concatenated Multiples in DT (Daily Test) in SkillRack in Python - Programming

Code :-

N , X = map(int , input().strip().split())

values_list = list(map(int , input().strip().split()))

count = sum(1 for i in range(0 , N) for j in range(0 , N) if((i != j) and ((int((str(values_list[i]) + str(values_list[j]))) % X) == 0)))

print(count)
