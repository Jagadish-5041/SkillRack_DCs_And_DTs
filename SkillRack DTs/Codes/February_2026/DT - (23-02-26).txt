Problem Statement :-

Add Two Integers - Form K in DT (Daily Test) in SkillRack in Python - Programming

Code :-

N , K = map(int , input().split())

values_list = list(map(int , input().split()))

count = sum(1 for index_1 in range(0 , N) for index_2 in range((index_1 + 1) , N) if(any((((values_list[index_1] * sign_value_1) + (values_list[index_2] * sign_value_2)) == K) for sign_value_1 in (-1 , 1) for sign_value_2 in (-1 , 1))))

print(count)
