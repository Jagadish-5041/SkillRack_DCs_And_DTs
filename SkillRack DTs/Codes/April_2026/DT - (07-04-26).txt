Problem Statement :-

Largest Integer In Each Column in DT (Daily Test) in SkillRack in Python - Programming

Code :-

R , C = map(int , input().split())

matrix = [list(map(int , input().split())) for i in range(0 , R)]

[print(max(matrix[i][j] for i in range(0 , R)) , end = " ") for j in range(0 , C)]
