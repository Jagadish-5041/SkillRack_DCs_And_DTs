Problem Statement :-

Swap Elements At Position X And Y in DT (Daily Test) in SkillRack in Python - Programming

Code :-

N , position_X , position_Y = map(int , input().split())

values_list = list(map(int , input().split()))

values_list[(position_X - 1)] , values_list[(position_Y - 1)] = values_list[(position_Y - 1)] , values_list[(position_X - 1)]

print(*values_list)
