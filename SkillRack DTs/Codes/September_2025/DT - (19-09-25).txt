Problem Statement :-

All Possible Concatenation in DT (Daily Test) in SkillRack in Python - Programming

Code :-

A = input().strip()

B = input().strip()

R_A = A[::-1]

R_B = B[::-1]

print(*sorted([(A + B) , (A + R_B) , (R_A + B) , (R_A + R_B) , (B + A) , (B + R_A) , (R_B + A) , (R_B + R_A)]) , sep = "\n")
