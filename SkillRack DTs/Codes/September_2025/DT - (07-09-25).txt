Problem Statement :-

Bar Graph Pattern - Custom Width in DT (Daily Test) in SkillRack in Python - Programming

Code :-

N = int(input())

lengths_list = list(map(int , input().split()))

width = int(input())

R = max(lengths_list)

C = (N * width)

graph_matrix = [['-' for j in range(0 , C)] for i in range(0 , R)]

for length , j in zip(lengths_list , range(0 , (C - width + 1) , width)):
    
    for column_index in range(j , (j + width)):
        
        for row_index in range((R - length) , R):
            
            graph_matrix[row_index][column_index] = '*'

[print(*row_list , sep = "") for row_list in graph_matrix]
