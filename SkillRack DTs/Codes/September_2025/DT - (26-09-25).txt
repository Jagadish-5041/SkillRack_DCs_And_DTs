Problem Statement :-

Expand Streets - City Map in DT (Daily Test) in SkillRack in Python - Programming

Code :-

R , C = map(int , input().split())

matrix = [list(input().strip()) for i in range(0 , R)]

K = int(input())

for i in range(0 , R):
    
    for j in range(0 , C):
        
        if(matrix[i][j] == '|'):
            
            matrix[i][j] = ('|' * K)

[print(*row_list , sep = "") for row_list in matrix]
