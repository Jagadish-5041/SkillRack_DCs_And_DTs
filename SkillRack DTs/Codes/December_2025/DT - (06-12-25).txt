Problem Statement :-

Find Length - Smallest Subarrays in DT (Daily Test) in SkillRack in Python - Programming

Code :-

N = int(input())

A = list(map(int , input().split()))

B = list(map(int , input().split()))

indices_list = [[j for j in range(i , N) if(A[j] >= A[i])] for i in range(0 , N)]

[(print(((-1) if(len(indices_list[index]) < B[index]) else (indices_list[index][(B[index] - 1)] - indices_list[index][0] + 1)) , end = " ")) for index in range(0 , N)]
