Problem Statement :-

Remove Preceding Alphabets And Sort in DT (Daily Test) in SkillRack in Python - Programming

Code :-

N = int(input())

words_list = [input().strip() for i in range(0 , N)]

shortest_word = min(words_list , key = len)

common_starting_character = ""

for character in shortest_word:
    
    if(sum(1 for word in words_list if((word != shortest_word) and (character in word))) == (N - 1)):
        
        common_starting_character = character
        
        break

print(*sorted([word[word.index(common_starting_character) : ] for word in words_list]) , sep = "\n")
