Problem Statement :-

Book - Numbering Subsections in DT (Daily Test) in SkillRack in Python - Programming

Code :-

N = int(input())

matrix = [[int(input().strip("-")) for j in range(0 , int(input().strip("-")))] for i in range(0 , N)]

[print((i + 1) , (j + 1) , (k + 1) , sep = ".") for i in range(0 , N) for j in range(0 , len(matrix[i])) for k in range(0 , matrix[i][j])]
