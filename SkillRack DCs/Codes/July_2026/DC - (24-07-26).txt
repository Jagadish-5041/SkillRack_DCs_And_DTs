Problem Statement :-

Row With The Most Unique Characters in DC (Daily Challenge) in SkillRack in Python - Programming

Code :-

N = int(input().strip())

row_wise_unique_characters_count_list = [len(set(input().strip().split())) for i in range(0 , N)]

maximum_unique_characters_count = max(row_wise_unique_characters_count_list)

last_most_row_position_with_maximum_unique_characters = [(index + 1) for index , unique_characters_count in enumerate(row_wise_unique_characters_count_list) if(unique_characters_count == maximum_unique_characters_count)][-1]

print(last_most_row_position_with_maximum_unique_characters)
