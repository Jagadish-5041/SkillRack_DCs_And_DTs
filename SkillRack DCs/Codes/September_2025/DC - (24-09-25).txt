Problem Statement :-

Print Frequency - Right To Left in DC (Daily Challenge) in SkillRack in Python - Programming

Code :-

S = input().strip()

frequency_map = {}

[frequency_map.__setitem__(character , (frequency_map.get(character , 0) + 1)) for character in S]

for character in S:
    
    print(character , frequency_map[character] , sep = "" , end = " ")
    
    frequency_map[character] -= 1
