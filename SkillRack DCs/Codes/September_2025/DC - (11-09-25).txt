Flowchart Logic 048 in DC (Daily Challenge) in SkillRack in Python - Programming

Code :-

X , Y = map(int , input().split())

while((X > 0) or (Y > 0)):
    
    if(X > 0):
        print(X , end = " ")
        Y -= 1
    
    if(Y > 0):
        print(Y , end = " ")
        X -= 1
    
    X -= 1
    
    Y -= 1
