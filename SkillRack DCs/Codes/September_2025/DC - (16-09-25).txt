Problem Statement :-

Flowchart Logic 050 in DC (Daily Challenge) in SkillRack in Python - Programming 

Code :-

M , N = map(int , input().split())

P = 0

while((M > 0) or (N > 0)):
    
    unit_digit_1 = (M % 10)
    
    unit_digit_2 = (N % 10)
    
    if(unit_digit_1 != unit_digit_2):
        
        print(max(unit_digit_1 , unit_digit_2) , end = "")
        
        P = 1
    
    M //= 10
    
    N //= 10

(print(-1)) if(P == 0) else ('')
