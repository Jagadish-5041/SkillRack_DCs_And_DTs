Flowchart Logic 056 in DC (Daily Challenge) in SkillRack in Python - Programming

Code :-

M , N = map(int , input().split())

ctr = 1

while((M > 0) or (N > 0)):
    
    if((ctr % 2) != 0):
        print((N % 10) , end = "")
        N //= 10
    else:
        print((M % 10) , end = "")
        M //= 10
    
    ctr += 1
