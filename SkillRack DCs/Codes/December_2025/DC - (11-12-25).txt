Problem Statement :-

Common Characters - Submatrices in DC (Daily Challenge) in SkillRack in Python - Programming

Code :-

R , C = map(int , input().split())

matrix = [input().strip().split() for i in range(0 , R)]

K = int(input())

common_characters_set = set()

flag = True

for i in range(0 , (R - K + 1) , K):
    
    for j in range(0 , (C - K + 1) , K):
        
        current_characters_set = set(matrix[k][l] for k in range(i , (i + K)) for l in range(j , (j + K)))
        
        common_characters_set = (current_characters_set) if(flag) else (common_characters_set & current_characters_set)
        
        flag = False

(lambda common_characters_count : print(common_characters_count)) (len(common_characters_set))
