Problem Statement :-

Increasing And Decreasing Window Series in DT (Daily Challenge) in SkillRack in Python - Programming

Code :-

N , K = map(int , input().split())

series_list = []

series_value = 1

increase_or_decrease_flag = 1

while(len(series_list) < K):
    
    current_series_list = []
    
    for count in range(1 , (N + 1)):
        
        current_series_list.append(series_value)
        
        series_value += 1
    
    series_list = (series_list + ((current_series_list) if(increase_or_decrease_flag == 1) else (current_series_list[::-1])))
    
    increase_or_decrease_flag = (increase_or_decrease_flag ^ 1)

print(series_list[(K - 1)])
