Problem Statement :-

Integers - Divide And Add Remainders in DC (Daily Challenge) in SkillRack in Python - Programming

Code :-

N = int(input())

elements_list = list(map(int , input().split()))

K = int(input())

K_elements_list = elements_list[0 : K]

remaining_elements_list = elements_list[K : N]

for value_1 in K_elements_list:
    
    for index_2 , value_2 in enumerate(remaining_elements_list):
        
        remaining_elements_list[index_2] += (value_2 % value_1)

print(*(K_elements_list + remaining_elements_list))
