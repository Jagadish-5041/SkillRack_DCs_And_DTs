Problem Statement :-

Integers - Strictly Increasing Or Not in DC (Daily Challenge) in SkillRack in Python - Programming

Code :-

N = int(input())

data_list = sorted([(lambda input_string : (int(input_string[ : -1]) , input_string[-1])) (input().strip()) for i in range(0 , N)] , key = lambda pair : pair[1].lower())

print(('Yes') if(all((data_list[i][0] < data_list[(i + 1)][0]) for i in range(0 , (N - 1)))) else ('No'))
