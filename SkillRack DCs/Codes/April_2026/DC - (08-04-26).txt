Problem Statement :-

Matrix - Sum Of Edge Elements in DC (Daily Challenge) in SkillRack in Python - Programming

Code :-

R , C = map(int , input().split())

matrix = [list(map(int , input().split())) for i in range(0 , R)]

print(sum(matrix[i][j] for i in range(0 , R) for j in range(0 , C) if((i == 0) or (i == (R - 1)) or (j == 0) or (j == (C - 1)))))
