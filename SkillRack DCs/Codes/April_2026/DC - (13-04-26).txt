Problem Statement :-

Train Journey - Initial Boarding Count in DC (Daily Challenge) in SkillRack in Python - Programming

Code :-

N , B = map(int , input().split())

initial_boarding_count = (B * -1)

for i in range(0 , N):
    
    passengers_who_alighted_the_train_count , passengers_who_boarded_the_train_count = map(int , input().split())
    
    initial_boarding_count = (initial_boarding_count + (passengers_who_alighted_the_train_count * - 1) + passengers_who_boarded_the_train_count)

print(abs(initial_boarding_count))
