Problem Statement :-

Bar Graph Pattern in DC (Daily Challenge) in SkillRack in Python - Programming

Code :-

N = int(input())

bar_graphs_lengths_list = list(map(int , input().split()))

R = max(bar_graphs_lengths_list)

C = N

matrix = [['-' for j in range(0 , C)] for i in range(0 , R)]

[matrix[i].__setitem__(j , '*') for j in range(0 , C) for i in range((R - bar_graphs_lengths_list[j]) , R)]

[print(*row_list , sep = "") for row_list in matrix]
