Problem Statement :-

Circular Track To Reach Initial Position in DC (Daily Challenge) in SkillRack in Python - Programming

Code :-

N , initial_position , M = map(int , input().split())

steps_count = 0

current_position = initial_position

while(True):
    
    steps_count = (steps_count + 1)
    
    current_position = (((current_position + M - 1) % N) + 1)
    
    if(current_position == initial_position):
        
        print(steps_count)
        
        quit()
