Problem Statement :-

Vertically Balanced Matrix Or Not in DC (Daily Challenge) in SkillRack in Python - Programming

Code :-

R , C = map(int , input().split())

matrix = [list(map(int , input().split())) for i in range(0 , R)]

total_sum = sum(sum(matrix[i]) for i in range(0 , R))

running_column_sum = 0

for j in range(0 , C):
    
    current_column_sum = sum(matrix[i][j] for i in range(0 , R))
    
    running_column_sum += current_column_sum
    
    left_half_sum = running_column_sum
    
    right_half_sum = (total_sum - left_half_sum)
    
    if(left_half_sum == right_half_sum):
        print("YES")
        quit()

print("NO")
