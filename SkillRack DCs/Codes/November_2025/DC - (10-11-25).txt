Problem Statement :-

Sum Of First N Terms in DC (Daily Challenge) in SkillRack in Python - Programming

Code :-

N = int(input())

M = 1

series_list = []

while(len(series_list) < N):
    
    [series_list.append(number) for number in range(1 , (M + 1))]
    
    M += 1

print(sum(series_list[ : N]))
