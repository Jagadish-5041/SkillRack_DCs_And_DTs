Problem Statement :-

Father & Son - Find Happiness in DC (Daily Challenge) in SkillRack in Python - Programming

Code :-

N = int(input())

chocolate_counts_list = list(map(int , input().split()))

X = chocolate_counts_list[0]

Y = chocolate_counts_list[0]

H = 0

F = 0

S = 0

for chocolates_count in chocolate_counts_list:
    
    if(chocolates_count > X):
        H += 1
    elif(chocolates_count < Y):
        S += 1
    else:
        F += 1
    
    X = max(X , chocolates_count)
    
    Y = min(Y , chocolates_count)

print(H , F , S)
