Problem Statement :-

Rearrange Matrix Without Duplicates In Each Column in DC (Daily Challenge) in SkillRack in Python - Programming

Code :-

R , C = map(int , input().strip().split())

matrix = [list(map(int , input().strip().split())) for i in range(0 , R)]

frequency_map = {}

for i in range(0 , R):
    
    for j in range(0 , C):
        
        frequency_map[matrix[i][j]] = (frequency_map.get(matrix[i][j] , 0) + 1)
        
        if(frequency_map[matrix[i][j]] > C):
            
            print("NO")
            
            quit()

print("YES")
